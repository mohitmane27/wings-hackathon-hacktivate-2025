Frequently Asked Questions

What is resume parsing?

Resume parsing is the process of converting an unstructured resume document into structured data that can be easily analyzed and stored. This technology automatically extracts information like contact details, work experience, education, and skills from resumes in various formats.

How do I parse my resume?

You can parse your resume by uploading it to our platform. We support various file formats including PDF, DOCX, and TXT. Once uploaded, our AI-powered parser will automatically extract and organize your information into a structured format.

How does resume parsing work?

Resume parsing works through a combination of OCR (Optical Character Recognition), NLP (Natural Language Processing), and machine learning algorithms. The system first extracts text from the document, then identifies sections like education and work history, and finally categorizes specific information within each section.

How does resume parsing help recruiters?

Resume parsing helps recruiters by automating the time-consuming process of manual data entry. It enables faster candidate screening, easier searchability of candidate information, improved candidate matching to job requirements, and standardized data for comparison across applications.

Is my data secure when using the resume parser?

Yes, your data is secure. We employ industry-standard encryption protocols for data transmission and storage. We don't share your information with third parties without your consent, and you retain full ownership of your data. Our systems are regularly audited for security compliance.
